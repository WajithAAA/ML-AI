My Projects



AI Chatbot

An AI chatbot is a computer program that simulates conversation with human users. It can be used for various purposes such as customer service, entertainment, or information gathering. AI chatbots can handle a large volume of interactions and understand and respond to a wide range of language. They can be integrated into different platforms and used for entertainment or education.

Stock Price Prediction

Stock price prediction is the use of machine learning algorithms to analyze historical stock data and make predictions about future stock price movements. A machine learning model is trained on a dataset of historical stock data and tested to evaluate its accuracy. Once trained, the model can be used to make predictions about future stock prices to help investors make informed decisions.

Cat & Dog Classifier

A cat and dog classifier is a machine learning model that is trained to identify and classify images of cats and dogs. It is created by collecting and preprocessing a dataset of labeled images, training a machine learning algorithm on the data, and testing the model to evaluate its accuracy. The classifier can then be used to classify new images of cats and dogs.
